About SDSS

Sri Durga Security & Allied Services (SDSS) is a professionally managed security and facility services company headquartered in DLF Phase-1, Gurgaon, Haryana. Founded and led by K.D. YADAV (formerly Chief Manager & Branch Head of SIS in DLF City, Gurgaon for over 10 years ) having looked after plotted areas and the prestigious Golf Course Project of DLF, SDSS has built a strong reputation for delivering reliable, affordable, and round-the-clock security solutions to residential societies, corporate offices, shopping malls, hospitals, industries, and construction sites across the NCR region.

Our personnel are police-verified, background-checked, uniformed, and trained to handle real-world security challenges with discipline and professionalism. We believe that true security is not just about presence — it's about preparedness, punctuality, and trust.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, professional, and round-the-clock security and facility management services that protect lives, property, and business operations across the NCR region.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted security service provider in Delhi NCR by building a team of disciplined, well-trained personnel who deliver consistent quality and zero-compromise safety.

Our Services

Society Security Guards

Trained and verified guards for residential societies, gated communities, apartments and housing complexes. 24x7 patrol, visitor management, and entry/exit monitoring.

Commercial & Office Security

Professional unarmed guards for corporate offices, IT parks, and business buildings in Gurgaon DLF and NCR. Shift-based deployment with daily reporting.

Mall & Retail Security

Crowd control, loss prevention, and emergency response for shopping malls, showrooms, and retail outlets. Weekend and festive season coverage available.

Housekeeping Services

Trained housekeeping staff for residential complexes, offices, malls, hospitals, and commercial buildings. Daily cleaning, deep cleaning, and waste management solutions.

Labour Supply

Skilled and unskilled contract labour for construction sites, factories, warehouses, and logistics. Background-verified workers available on flexible short-term and long-term contracts.

Industrial & Facility Management

End-to-end facility management including maintenance staff, pantry boys, office helpers, and sanitation teams for industrial and commercial premises.
