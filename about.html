About Sri Durga Security & Allied Services

Gurgaon's Trusted Security & Facility Management Partner

Who We Are

Sri Durga Security & Allied Services (SDSS) is a professionally managed security and facility services company headquartered in DLF Phase-1, Gurgaon, Haryana. Founded and led by K.D. YADAV (formerly Chief Manager & Branch Head of SIS in DLF City, Gurgaon for over 10 years ) having looked after plotted areas and the prestigious Golf Course Project of DLF, SDSS has built a strong reputation for delivering reliable, affordable, and round-the-clock security solutions to residential societies, corporate offices, shopping malls, hospitals, industries, and construction sites across the NCR region.

Our personnel are police-verified, background-checked, uniformed, and trained to handle real-world security challenges with discipline and professionalism. We believe that true security is not just about presence — it's about preparedness, punctuality, and trust.
