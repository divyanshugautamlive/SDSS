About SDSS

Sri Durga Security & Allied Services (SDSS) is a trusted name in Security and Facility Management based in DLF Phase-1, Gurgaon, Haryana. Led by Saurav Yadav, we provide trained, verified, and professional staff for Residential Societies, Commercial Complexes, Malls, Industries, and Corporate Offices across the NCR region. Our commitment is to deliver reliable, affordable, and round-the-clock services to every client.

  • Police Verified & Background Checked Staff
  • 24x7 Availability & Support
  • Quick Deployment Across Gurgaon NCR
  • Trained, Uniformed & Professional Personnel

Our Services

Society Security Guards

Trained and verified guards for residential societies, gated communities, apartments and housing complexes. 24x7 patrol, visitor management, and entry/exit monitoring.

Commercial & Office Security

Professional unarmed guards for corporate offices, IT parks, and business buildings in Gurgaon DLF and NCR. Shift-based deployment with daily reporting.

Mall & Retail Security

Crowd control, loss prevention, and emergency response for shopping malls, showrooms, and retail outlets. Weekend and festive season coverage available.

Housekeeping Services

Trained housekeeping staff for residential complexes, offices, malls, hospitals, and commercial buildings. Daily cleaning, deep cleaning, and waste management solutions.

Labour Supply

Skilled and unskilled contract labour for construction sites, factories, warehouses, and logistics. Background-verified workers available on flexible short-term and long-term contracts.

Industrial & Facility Management

End-to-end facility management including maintenance staff, pantry boys, office helpers, and sanitation teams for industrial and commercial premises.
