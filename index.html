About SDSS

Sri Durga Security & Allied Services (SDSS) is a professional service organization providing security, manpower and maintenance services with its headquarter in DLF Phase-1, Gurgaon, Haryana. The firm is managed by Mr. K.D. YADAV, formerly Chief Manager & Branch Head of SIS in DLF City, Gurgaon for over 10 years & also former Chief Manager at SLV Security.

Having extensively looked after both commercial & residential areas in DLF City and the prestigious Golf Course Project of DLF, he has acquired exceptional sense of security operations, risk assessment and situational awareness and has built a strong reputation for delivering reliable and affordable security & maintenance solutions to both residential and commercial areas across the NCR region.

We do not merely make a business out of your security but we consider your security our responsibility.
